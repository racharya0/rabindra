    Projects

    Nyano Nani - Infant Radiant Warmer

    What?

    A NIC initiative, low cost infant radiant warmer designed to provide thermal support for newborns in the delivery suite as a means to protect hypothermia. Nyano Nani is particularly designed to fit in the nepalese socio-economical and healthcare scenario.

    How?

    Worked on identification of design assessment according to relevant standards: IEC 60601-1 and IEC 60601-2-21. My involvements started from rapid prototyping to drafting production drawings for final production. I also collaborated with TU Delft and MIT students to create a V2 of the base model.

    Results

    Nyano Nani successfully operates in more than 40 public hospitals around the country which also includes the busiest delivery ward of the capital state.

    Nyano Nani V2 is 29% more energy efficient, 35% lighter, and 55% smaller which will be more economical than it's predecessor.

    Mina - Autonomous Mobile Robot

    What?

    A prototype ROS based robot module that fits into the category of autonomous mobile robots, the application domain is focused but not limited to indoor application, an idea started as to facilitate contactless delivery of food and medicine during pandemic.

    How?

    Performed full product engineering design using SOLIDWORKS 3D CAD, ROS URDF integration, and rapid prototyping using SLA 3D Printing, FDM 3D Printing, laser cutting, plasma cutting, and other workshop technologies.

    Results

    A MVP prototype that was able to navigate around the premises of NIC, with object detection AI, along with an UI that can be operated with a touch display. A lighter prototype made out of aluminium and fiberglass.

    'Bini' - Waitress Concept

    What?

    A waitress humanoid concept based on ROS autonomous navigation, that embodies friendly gestures for seamless human robot interaction.

    How?

    Modeled on the concept using SOLIDWORKS 3D CAD, small platforms comprising depth sensor and LiDAR were made for mapping, and using the ROS 2 the humanoid was simulated in a custom world.

    Results

    Real-life navigation achieved. A starter robotics project which laid a foundation to my other projects.
